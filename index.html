Dinner and a Movie

Lately, we have been spending much more time at home. And with that, we have been watching more movies and making more food ourselves. Have you ever been stuck on what to watch or what to eat? Whether you're alone or with someone, you can't decide what to land on so you go with the same food and the same movie you've seen over and over. Well, we have a solution for you! Dinner and a Movie, where we pick what you eat and watch. No more date night fights, or the same boring meal. The user will start on our landing page which will make them hungry and excited based on our color scheme, and once they enter the site they will be brought here, with the name up top and a brief description and instructions. On the left they will have a drop down option for food type with a generate button, a dropdown option with movie genres and generate button, or they can generate both together randomly. On the right we will have a picture of the generated food, the title, list of ingredients and recipe instructions with a link to a video. And on the right of that we will have a movie poster with the ratings, description, where to watch link and year released. If you like one, but not the other, you can click the movie OR food generate button for a new exciting response.
